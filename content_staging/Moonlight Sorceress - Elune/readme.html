Description

@Rence Kristoff asked me to make a nice Night Elf Sorceress and enchance the one he saw. And I decided to make this one.

Could be Elune in her young days. (Yes... I dunno nothing about her nor her life...and yes...that's Soraka's Moon-Banana Staff)

Alternative Lore: "In her young days, Elune always as fascinated by the Moon and its arcane magic. Different from her sisters that were more Warrior-like. Her hard study lead her to a inner knowledge. Such knowledge defended her home in the first invasion of the Burning Legion, where Sargeras himself led the siege.

With a mighty call Elune summoned the High Moon to blast her enemies. Sargeras fleet in fear while injured. Since that day Elune was proclamed the Moonlight Goddess."

ABILITIES:

Passive - Moonlight Bolt: Elune's basic attacks drains 5% of her Current Mana to deal the same amount as additional Magic Damage.

Q - Starfall: Elune calls down might starlight meteors to strike nearby enemy units, dealing 50/85/120/155/190 (+45%AP) as Magic Damage.

Mana Cost: 50/60/70/80/90
Cooldown: 5 seconds in all Levels

W - Moonlight Orbs: Elune summons 3 magical orbs that circundates her and chase down nearby enemies, each one deals 80/120/160/200/240 (+50%AP) as Magic Damage. Enemies hit by additional orbs receive 50% less damage.

Mana Cost: 100 in all Levels
Cooldown: 14/13/12/11/10 seconds

E - Moonlight Shield: The High Moon smiles upon Elune, creating a shield that blocks 80/100/120/140/160 (+65%AP). The shield lasts 10 seconds and upon breaking ONLY, the shield deals the same amount plus 1% per 1% of Elune's Current Mana as Magic Damage.

Mana Cost: 80/90/100/110/120
Cooldown: 20/19/18/17/16 seconds

R - Moonlight Blast: Elune calls the mighty High Moon that once inflicted high damage to Sargeras. Elune locks the targeted enemy Hero in a runic prision for 3 seconds, while the enemy is locked it cannot be targeted nor damaged and Elune starts CHANNELING a powerful energy for the same amount of time.

After 3 seconds the High Moon sends a Moonlight Blast against her target, dealing 200/350/500 (+40%AP) (+10%Maximum Mana) as True Damage and stuns the target for the same 3 seconds.

Mana Cost: 200/300/400
Cooldown: 300/265/230 seconds

Keywords: Elune, Moonlight, Sorceress, Night Elf, High, Moon, Sargeras
