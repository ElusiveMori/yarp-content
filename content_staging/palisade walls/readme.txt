The map features following:

   - fully walkable walls with realistic heights

   - edges and corners to fill the gap between the walls

   - tiles, basically planes wiht a tiling texture

   - fully walkable stairs, since they are destructibles and "walkable" is ticked

   - everything else walkable with invisible platforms, the models dont stick to each other 
     very well when used as destructible



NOTE: Stairs and gate are destructibles, otherwise you could not give the gate animation advice 
      and the stairs are better walkable this way.
